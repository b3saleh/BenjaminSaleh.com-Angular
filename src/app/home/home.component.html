BackBeat

BackBeat is a performance drum machine designed for musicians and buskers.
Tap sensors are used to help you record your drum beat, a micro-controller will continuously repeat the beat exactly how you played it and finally, a surface actuator will use your the body of your guitar as an amplifier to play your beat as you play your song. Battery powered and compact design allows you to take it anywhere.
